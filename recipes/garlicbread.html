Cheesy Garlic Bread

This cheesy garlic bread is as simple and straightforward as a cheesy garlic bread should be. Serve with your favorite pasta dish or with soup.

Ingredients

  • Loaf of French Bread
  • Unsalted butter, melted
  • Garlic powder
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Preheat oven and place slices of bread. Brush melted butter on top.
  2. Divide garlic powder, Parmesan and Mozzarella between slices.
  3. Bake and then sprinkle with cilantro.
